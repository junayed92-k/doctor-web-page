About Dr. Sarah Johnson

With over 15 years of experience in family medicine, Dr. Sarah Johnson is dedicated to providing comprehensive, compassionate healthcare to patients of all ages.

She graduated from Harvard Medical School and completed her residency at Johns Hopkins Hospital. Dr. Johnson is board-certified in Family Medicine and is committed to staying current with the latest medical advances.

Her approach focuses on preventive care, patient education, and building lasting relationships with her patients and their families.
